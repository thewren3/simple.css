About

The Upper Valley Running Segment Challenge is based off of a similar challenge that happened in January, where runners in different cities competed against each other by running a specific “segment” as many times as possible. You can read more about this challenge here – it was very fun to follow along! I thought that this challenge would be fun to bring to the upper valley as it rewards distance and time on feet, not as much speed, which is a fun twist on racing.

For our Upper Valley edition, we will adapt the rules a little to encourage wider participation. But the gist of the competition is: every segment completion nets you 1 point. Whoever has the most points at the end of the competition wins.

Everyone is welcome and encouraged to participate! You do not need to have Strava; all you have to have is some way of tracking your run (Garmin, nikerun, apple, coros, etc.)

The Segments

There are 4 segments in the challenge, designed as such to maximize accessibility. All of these segments count for the same, which means that each of these, when completed, count for 1 point.

Segment 1: Pine Park mini loop

Segment 2: Beaver Meadow Sprint

Segment 3: Rail Trail, Covered Bridge to Pool

Segment 4: Hartford Ave to Watson Park

Rules

Competition Guidelines:

  • Segments are only one direction. There is an established start point, and an established finish point. So if you complete a segment and then run that segment back the way you came, this would still only count as one segment completion.

  • One segment completion equals one point. You may complete a segment as many times as you want within a run (and thus you can net multiple points all within one run). You may also complete multiple segments in one run.

  • Segment completions must be done on foot. No vehicle assistance allowed.

  • Segment completions have to be proven; to prove you’ve finished a segment, you can either submit a link to the strava/garmin/coros activity, or link to a shared google doc with a picture/screenshot of the run details. Docs should be shared with uppervalleyrunningsegment@gmail.com

  • Segment completions should be submitted within 5 days of completion. In the last week, May 24th-May 31st, submissions should be within 24 hours of completion.

  • Anyone and everyone is welcome to participate! Spread the word to your friends, family, and neighbors!

  • This challenge’s time frame is from May 1st to May 31st, 11:59pm
